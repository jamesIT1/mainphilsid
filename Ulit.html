About Us

PhilSid Concept & Design Commercial Inc. is a dynamic and innovative affiliate of World Solution Technology Inc., specializing in office design, renovation, and the provision of high-quality furniture solutions. With a commitment to delivering exceptional design concepts and top-notch furniture products, we empower businesses, schools, hotels, and individuals to create functional and aesthetically pleasing environments that inspire productivity and comfort.

Our experienced team, consisting of professionals with diverse backgrounds in designs, furniture sales, and project management, can turn your office design dreams into reality. We stay updated on the latest trends and timeless classics in office construction to provide innovative solutions tailored to your needs.
